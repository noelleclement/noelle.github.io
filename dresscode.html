Dresscode

I can hear you thinking: "What? Dresscode? What would that be? Did I click on the wrong link? "
Nope, this is the page where I tell you all about the awesome stuff I do with Dresscode: the organization for female IT-students at my university!
"Sounds cool Noelle, but what do you guys (oops, I mean girls) do?"
Well, this what I have done in the past year:
  • organize events for young girls, to show them how much fun coding is
  • give workshops at events (like CodeEvent, organized in collaboration with VHTO)
  • speak at events (like the kick-off of a project about the computational thinking of teachers, organized by Google and VHTO!)
  • help at open days of the university and study, to show future students that there are actual female computer engineers. The percentage of women in the IT-studies at our university actually increased, so that may show that it actually works :)
  • organize team building activities. As a team building activity I decided that it would be fun to go to an escape room with some of the Dresscoders. It was a lot of organizing but so much fun in the end!
  • have a drink every once in a while and talk about life
