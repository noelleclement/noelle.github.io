Events

I really like attending and helping at events. Most of them are tech-related, since I get asked mostly by teachers or other students to attend. Here are some of them:

HBO-I - Sept 2017

HBO-I is an annual career fair in Rotterdam at which a lot of IT-companies try to recruit students. Not only is there a fair, but there were also a lot of interesting masterclasses given by said companies. I was asked to help out at the event and meanwhile I got to talk to a lot of companies and students. I even recognized myself in a photo, taken at the Hackathon spoken about below :)

Women in IT Hackathon - Jun 2017

Previous Next
On my birthday I got the chance to attend a hackathon, organized by Caesar Experts. It was a women-only hackathon, focused on trying to create a real-life Mario Kart experience, with the HoloLens! Not only was it fun, but I also learned a lot and was interviewed about the need for more women in the technology field. A (Dutch) video of the hackathon and this interview can be seen here

LAN-party - May 2017

The study association organized a great LAN party, which included lots of casual and retro games, fortunately for me :)

Nerds on Stage - Nov 2016

The two other girls in my year and I were asked by our university to help at an event called 'Nerds on Stage'. This event was focused on showing high schoolers how fun and versatile technology is.
